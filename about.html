Our Story

Founded in 2018 by a team of financial experts and technology innovators, Veloxtrades emerged with a clear mission: to democratize access to sophisticated investment strategies that were previously only available to institutional investors.

What started as a small fintech startup in London has grown into a global investment platform serving over 50,000 investors across 80+ countries. Our journey has been fueled by a relentless focus on innovation, security, and client success.
